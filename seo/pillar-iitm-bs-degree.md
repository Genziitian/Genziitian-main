## BODY DRAFT

### Direct answer (first 50–60 words — featured-snippet target)

The **IIT Madras BS Degree** is a UGC-recognised, fully online Bachelor's in **Data Science and Applications** offered by IIT Madras. Anyone who has passed Class 12 can join — there's no entrance exam, only a 4‑week **Qualifier**. You progress through four levels — **Qualifier → Foundation → Diploma → BSc/BS Degree** — earning credits at your own pace.

> _Student initiative — GenZ IITian is **not officially affiliated with IIT Madras**. Always cross-check dates and rules on the official IITM BS site._
